# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Short-form creators, social media editors, students, founders, and solo makers who need to turn rough phone clips into polished captioned videos without opening a heavyweight editor.

## Product Purpose

Captrix helps users upload a 30 second to 2 minute clip, add captions, style them with creator-friendly motion, and export a caption kit that can be shared or reused.

## Brand Personality

Precise, kinetic, creator-native.

## Anti-references

Captrix should not look like a generic AI wrapper, a testing dashboard, a beige SaaS template, or a toy meme generator.

## Design Principles

- Put the clip first; every control should support the preview.
- Make states visible and recoverable.
- Keep the core flow fast: upload, caption, style, export.
- Use AI as a local assist, not as a black-box dependency.
- Design for proof: every important flow should be easy for TestSprite to verify.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, visible keyboard focus, logical tab order, touch targets of at least 44px, and reduced-motion alternatives for caption animations.
